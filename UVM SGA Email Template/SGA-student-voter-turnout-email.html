It Matters — Help Us Organize

Although broad-based volunteering is the engine of this movement, student organizers make sure everyone is on the same page and help us increase the reach of our work across campus. Because text-banking depends on peers already knowing each other, we see the best results when we reach the least represented segments of campus.
